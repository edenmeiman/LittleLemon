Who we are

Little Lemon is owned by two Italian siblings, Maria and Adriano, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant. To craft the menu, Maria relies on family recipes and her experience as a chef in Italy. Adriano does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Italian to incorporate additional cuisines from the Mediterranean region.

The Restaurant

Based in Manhattan, New-York, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist. The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day. The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12–15 items that they rotate seasonally.
